Construction Process

Our Home Construction Steps are Simple and Easy to Understand

  1. Place an Enquiry
  2. You will get a call from us to understand your requirements
  3. Our Project team and Architect will Vist your Site
  4. We will provide you a Quotation with detailed Specification & Payment Terms
  5. Sign the Construction Ageement with a Booking Advance
  6. We will start making Architectural Drawings & 3D Designs
  7. We will Start the work. Sit & Relax, while we build your Home. Track the work progress in APP
